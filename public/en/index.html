About me

Who am I?

With over 12 years of experience across media, banking, insurance, and retail, I've evolved into a specialist in crafting intuitive interfaces and meaningful user experiences for both mobile and web applications. My expertise spans front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, Sass, JavaScript, React) and design tools (Sketch, Photoshop, After Effects, InDesign, Final Cut Pro). Currently, I'm exploring how AI is reshaping design practices and creating new possibilities for user-centered experiences.

Throughout my career, I've both participated in and led multidisciplinary design and development teams. Within Product Design, I've conducted extensive user research to build comprehensive data frameworks that illuminate genuine user needs and desires. This evidence-based approach enables me to craft engaging user experiences that significantly enhance how people interact with digital products, driving both user satisfaction and business outcomes.

My professional vision extends beyond immediate projects—I'm committed to advancing my career through innovative solutions that tangibly improve people's lives through thoughtful, accessible design. Every digital interaction presents an opportunity to create a more intuitive, delightful experience.

What I Do

Web Design

I create attractive, accessible interfaces for websites and web applications that balance visual appeal with intuitive functionality. Each design prioritizes user needs while supporting key business objectives through thoughtful information architecture and responsive layouts.

User Flow & Wireframing

I build logical, efficient structures that naturally guide users through digital products. By mapping complete journeys and wireframing key touchpoints, I develop frameworks that deliver intuitive, satisfying experiences that reduce cognitive load and increase engagement.

Visual Design

I leverage the power of color, typography, imagery, and visual hierarchy to create cohesive, emotionally resonant digital experiences. My visual design approach balances aesthetic appeal with functional clarity to reinforce brand identity while enhancing usability across all touchpoints.

Mobile App Design

I design mobile experiences that combine intuitive interaction patterns with compelling visual treatments to create memorable, effective applications. My approach considers the specific constraints and opportunities of each device to deliver experiences that feel natural, responsive, and delightful on smaller screens.

UX & UI Design

Through user research, wireframing, prototyping, and strategic testing, I create coherent digital experiences that resonate with users on both practical and emotional levels. My approach balances business objectives with user needs to deliver solutions that feel intuitive, meaningful, and delightful to use.
