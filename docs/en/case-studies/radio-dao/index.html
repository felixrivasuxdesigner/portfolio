Context

A historic community radio station facing stagnation, transformed into a DAO to democratize ownership and modernize its reach.

The Challenge

Radio Barrio Centenario, a historic pillar of the community, was facing generational and technological stagnation. Its traditional transmission model (FM) limited its geographic reach, and decision-making was centralized, pushing away new voices seeking to participate. The challenge was not just to digitize the signal, but to democratize ownership and management of the medium, transforming passive listeners into active members with a voice and vote.

My Approach

Discovery & Inspiration

I started with a deep immersion in the community, conducting interviews with historic founders and workshops with young local creators. I discovered that the visual identity needed to evoke warmth and tradition, but the infrastructure had to be radically modern and transparent. Inspiration came from the fusion of the "analog" (human voice, history) and the "digital" (blockchain, global streaming).

Experience Definition

I designed an information architecture centered on three fundamental pillars, guaranteeing that the technological complexity of a DAO was invisible to the end user:

  • Governance (DAO): A simplified voting system where community members use participation "tokens" to decide programming and elect directives.
  • Hybrid Streaming: Low-latency audio playback accessible from any mobile connection, with on-demand podcasting capabilities to preserve historical memory.
  • Living Community: Real-time interaction spaces (chat, reactions) integrated directly into the listening experience, eliminating the need for external social networks for immediate feedback.

Technical Implementation

I led the experience design for a multi-platform ecosystem:

  • Streaming: Integration of Icecast protocols optimized for unstable mobile networks.
  • Invisible Web3: Authentication via simplified wallets (account abstraction) so users vote on the blockchain without managing complex seed phrases.
  • Native Apps: Development of Web App and Mobile App (iOS/Android) ready for deployment, ensuring visual and behavioral consistency.
  • Optimization: Performance in audio loading and data consumption was prioritized, implementing aggressive caching strategies for static content and adaptive compression for the audio stream.
